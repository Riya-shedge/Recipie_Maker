Chocolate Cake

  • 1 ¾ cups : Flour
  • 1 ½ cups : Sugar
  • ¾ cup : Cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp : Baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp : Baking soda
  • 1 tsp : Salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup : Milk
  • ½ cup : Oil
  • 2 tsp : Vanilla extract
  • 1 cup : Water

Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans or line them with parchment paper.

Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

Add Wet Ingredients: Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix well until smooth.

Add Boiling Water: Stir in the boiling water until the batter becomes smooth and thin.

Bake the Cake: Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Cool the Cakes: Allow the cakes to cool for 10 minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.

Frost the Cake: Once the cakes are cool, frost them with your favorite frosting (chocolate buttercream works great).

Add your favorite toppings: Make your cake more attractive with chocolates or biscuits or donuts and enjoy the cake with your family.

Preparation Time

45:00 mins

Serving Size

5-8 people
